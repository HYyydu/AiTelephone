## ğŸ’¡ Tips for Best Results

### 1. **Be Specific in Purpose**
- âŒ Bad: "Call about a problem"
- âœ… Good: "I bought shoes online but received the wrong size. I need to exchange size 8 for size 9."

### 2. **Provide Realistic Context**
- Include order numbers, dates, account details
- Makes the AI's responses more believable
- Example: "Order #12345 placed on March 1st"

### 3. **Set Clear Expectations**
- What outcome does the customer want?
- How should they react to different responses?
- Example: "If they offer refund, accept. If only store credit, negotiate."

### 4. **Match Tone to Situation**
- Complaint â†’ Firm but polite
- Inquiry â†’ Friendly and curious  
- Urgent issue â†’ Concerned but professional

### 5. **Test Edge Cases**
- AI stays calm when CS rep is unhelpful
- AI provides additional details when asked
- AI accepts reasonable solutions
- AI escalates if needed
